    Deal With It!

    • $DEAL is a memecoin on the WAXP blockchain with hyperdeflationary tokenomics. It features a 4% burn on each transaction.

    • On each transaction, 4% of the transaction value is permanently burned, reducing the total supply of $DEAL over time.

    • $DEAL can be purchased using $WAXP on Wax On Edge and other participating exchanges.

    • As with any cryptocurrency, investing in $DEAL carries risks. Its hyperdeflationary tokenomics and memecoin nature make it highly speculative.
